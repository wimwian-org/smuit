Add `@smuit/progress-indicator` (v1) — a Material Design 3–style linear progress indicator built from the design spec on smuit design tokens. Ships determinate (`value` / `max`, the Material fraction model) and indeterminate (animated) modes, a retintable accent (neutral · primary · secondary · error · warning · success), three track heights (sm · md · lg), a `prefers-reduced-motion` fallback, and full `role="progressbar"` ARIA, with automatic light/dark theming. The circular shape, four-color mode, linear buffer, and a built-in label slot are deferred to a later release.
